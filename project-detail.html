Branding & Identity

Nova Branding

A new identity for a cosmic fintech giant

Challenge & Objective

Fintech branding is traditionally conservative, boring, and fails to engage Gen-Z and millennial investors. Nova wanted to break the mold while maintaining credibility and security.

Strategic Execution

We designed a full brand system inspired by deep space gravity. This included an updated logotype, custom orbit-themed iconography, a palette of hyper-violet and neon green, and a brand voice that is clear, bold, and futuristic.

Project Impact

The brand identity successfully elevated Nova's profile, making them the most discussed fintech startup of the quarter. Users highly praised the clean mobile app layout and the immersive onboarding animations, establishing them as industry leaders.
